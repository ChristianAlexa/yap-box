yap-box — Install

Requires an Apple Silicon Mac (M1/M2/M3/M4).

1. Drag yap-box.app to the Applications folder (shortcut included in this DMG).

2. Clear the macOS quarantine flag so Gatekeeper stops blocking the unsigned
   build. Open Terminal and run:

       xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/yap-box.app

   Alternative without a terminal: right-click yap-box.app -> Open -> Open
   in the warning dialog. On macOS 15 (Sequoia) right-click-Open may not
   bypass Gatekeeper -- open System Settings -> Privacy & Security and click
   "Open Anyway" next to the blocked-app message.

3. On first launch, yap-box prompts you to download the Kokoro-82M TTS model
   (~354 MB). No bytes are fetched until you click Download in the dialog.

Troubleshooting and full docs:
https://github.com/ChristianAlexa/yap-box#readme
